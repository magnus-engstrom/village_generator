Depending on how you look at it, this might be more of a mindfullness application rather than a game.

When you create a new landscape, either by uploading an image or by a using random seed, the virtual settlers will try to establish a village. There isn't really any way to win or loose in this game, but you will however get a population count and a "score". The score is calculated from the size of the population and the diversity of the enviroment you helped create. Every time you generate a new world you are looking at something unique, never before seen. Have fun.

Regarding privacy: Uploaded images used when generating a village are not stored in any way by the application. All image processing is done directly in the web browser and no image data is sent to the backend/server. Also, there are no cookies.
